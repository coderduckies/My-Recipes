Strawberry Milk

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 3 tbs sugar
  • 2 cups milk

Instructions

  1. Mush strawberries and sugar in saucepan.
  2. Bring to simmer over medium heat. Stir consistently.
  3. Cool strawberry syrup
  4. Pour syrup and chilled milk into glass. Enjoy!

Veggie Loco Moco

Ingredients

  • 1 Veggie Patty
  • Some tasty brown gravy
  • Some cooked brown rice
  • 1 Egg

Instructions

  1. Fry veggie patty until cooked to your liking. I like mine pretty crispy.
  2. Fry egg to desired done-ness.
  3. Heat up that instant Costco gravy.
  4. In an eating bowl, layer rice, patty, egg, then gravy. Stuff your gob and enjoy your 7th rewatch of The Office.

Crispy Quesadilla

Ingredients

  • A single corn tortilla
  • Some shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. Lightly oil pan. Warm torilla over medium-high heat. Flip when soft and floppy.
  2. Sprinkle that delicious shredded cheese on half of the tortilla.
  3. Fold tortilla, lifting the non-cheese side.
  4. Cook until crispy.
  5. Flip. Cook until crispy.
  6. Cool. Enjoy.
